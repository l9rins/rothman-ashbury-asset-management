The Cost of Capital

The devastating mathematics of management fees

In the world of investing, there is a fundamental law of physics that is rarely discussed: the devastating impact of compounding fees over long periods of time. Even a seemingly insignificant 1% or 2% annual management fee can confiscate the vast majority of an investor's absolute wealth over a multi-decade horizon.
