Important legal information

At Rothman Ashbury Asset Management Pty Ltd, we try to speak plainly about what we do — and just as plainly about what we don't do.

We are a private single family office, established solely to manage the financial affairs and assets of our owners. That is our entire purpose. We do not hold ourselves out as advisers, managers, promoters or product providers to anyone outside the family group and we have no intention of becoming one.

What We Do Not Do

To avoid any misunderstanding, here is what we do not do:

  • We do not provide financial product advice — not general, not personal, not in any form
  • We do not provide personal financial advice of any kind
  • We do not deal in financial products for others (only for our own family)
  • We do not operate, promote or offer a managed investment scheme
  • We do not solicit, accept or manage funds from external parties
  • We do not offer services to retail or wholesale clients
  • We do not invite, encourage or accept investment from anyone outside our ownership group

Everything on this website exists for one audience only: the family that owns the company.

If you are reading this and you are not part of the family group, you should understand that the content is not written for you and must not be relied upon.

Not Financial Advice

Nothing on this website should be taken as:
