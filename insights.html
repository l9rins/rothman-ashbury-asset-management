Insights

Thoughts, observations and lessons that matter to us as owners

Investing rewards clear thinking, steady temperament and a willingness to look past the noise of the moment. The notes and reflections below are our way of putting those habits into writing.

They are rooted in the lineage of thoughtful investors who came before us — from Graham's analytical foundations to Buffett's partnership principles, Munger's clarity of mind, and the enduring practices of owners who have proven that patience and rationality still work.

We publish these pieces not to predict markets or offer advice, but to clarify our own thinking and to help preserve a tradition of rational, long‑term investing in a world that often prefers speed over sense.
